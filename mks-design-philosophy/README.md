## Assets

- `assets/curated/` - Finalized images selected for the site build
- `assets/raw-imports/` - Drop images here. They will be renamed based on what they contain and moved/copied to curated when selected.

## Key Decisions Captured

- Contact page replaced with **Store**
- **Tour** section added
- No AI-generated artist imagery - authenticity is a core brand value
- Nature elements (ocean, sky, clouds, sunsets, horizons) are structural metaphors, not decoration
- Score sheets (handwritten music notation) are a unique visual asset and potential product
- The site should feel like an expensive art gallery, not a template
- Cinematic pacing throughout - slow reveals, deliberate motion
