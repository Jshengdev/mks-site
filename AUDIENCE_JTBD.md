# Audience & Jobs to Be Done

## Primary Audience

### The Listener Who Felt Something
People who heard Michael Kim-Sheng's music and had a visceral, physical emotional response — "it lifts my heart higher in my chest," "my ears strain to hear the next note." They want to RETURN to that feeling, inhabit it spatially, and share it.

### The Music Supervisor / Collaborator
Industry professionals evaluating MKS for film scoring, sync licensing, or collaboration. They need to feel the emotional range immediately — not read about it. The site IS the portfolio.

### The Curious Discoverer
Someone who landed via a link, social post, or recommendation. Zero context on who MKS is. The site must make them feel something within 10 seconds of entering, before they know anything about the artist.

## Jobs to Be Done

### JTBD 1: "Feel the music as a place I can inhabit"
When a listener enters the site, they want to experience the emotional world of MKS's music — not read about it. The meadow, the light, the wind, the fog — these are the FEELING made tangible.
**Activities:** Enter → hear music begin → scroll through atmospheric transformation → reach emotional peak → feel changed → want to explore more worlds

### JTBD 2: "Quickly assess emotional range for professional use"
When a music supervisor visits, they want to experience 2-3 emotional territories in under 60 seconds to know if MKS fits their project.
**Activities:** Land → navigate between worlds via MiniPlayer → each world demonstrates a distinct emotional register → decision made

### JTBD 3: "Share this experience with someone who needs to feel it"
When a listener finds a world that resonates, they want to send a URL that drops their friend directly into that feeling.
**Activities:** Deep link to specific world → recipient enters at the right moment → they feel it too

### JTBD 4: "Discover the artist behind the feeling"
After being emotionally moved, a visitor wants context — who made this? How can they hear more? Is there an album?
**Activities:** Content sections surface naturally during scroll → artist bio, album info, links appear at the right emotional moments → never interrupts the feeling
